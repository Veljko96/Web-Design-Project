Sava center

Sava Center, the largest congress, cultural and business center in our country and one of the largest in the region, is a modern center of exceptional domestic and international reputation whose activity includes the organization of congresses and all kinds of artistic events and events. Built in 1977, Sava Center is a modern complex located in one of the most beautiful parts of Belgrade, only five minutes from the city center and 15 minutes from the airport "Nikola Tesla". There are more Deluxe hotels in its immediate vicinity. One of them, Hotel Crowne Plaza is connected with the Sava Center with a glass pasarel and together with it makes a unique architectural space made up of three main parts: the business building - the "A" building, the large congressional concert hall - the "B" building and Hotel Crowne Plaza. Over the past three decades, Sava Center has been the venue of the largest international congressional meetings, including the CSCE, the General Assembly of the World Bank and the International Monetary Fund, the General Assembly of UNESCO, the 9th Summit of Non-Aligned Countries, the EBRD Assembly, the World Miners Congress, dentist, surgeon ... The Sava Center hosted the greatest names of the world art scene, and for many years there have been held important cultural events such as FEST, BEMUS, BITEF ... Clients and visitors of the Sava Center have at their disposal: renting business and commercial space, catering services and catering, exclusive shops and boutiques, art galleries, bookstores, banks, travel and rent-a-car agencies, internet, professional security service, parking .. . The Sava Center is a member of significant international congress and art associations, such as the International Congress and Convention Association (ICCA), the International Association of Congress Centers (ISPA), the International Society of Performing Arts (ISPA), the ILMC (International Live Music Conference) .
