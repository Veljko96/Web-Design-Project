Fountain

The Fountain Cinema was built in 1967, and after more than a decade of decay and oblivion, the cult site of the filmofil from the New Belgrade side of Sava was reopened in 2012 (on Women's Day). The Municipality of Novi Beograd initiated the reconstruction with the intention to develop the Center of European and Children's Film Cinema as a part of the Cinema Cinema, as an educational segment of film art that is missing in Novi Beograd and the City of Belgrade. Partners in the realization of European and children's film programs are Art & Popcorn Film Art Center and Kids Fest Children's Film Festival. While preserving the renown of the old singer, the mainstream is never the first choice, this cinema is primarily the home of European and domestic films, and gives special attention to achievements for children. In order to build a new base of viewers, the repertoire displays films that have the opportunity to see only at festivals, and an insight into European cinema is provided throughout the year. In addition to the regular cinema program, the retrospectives of European cinemas, as well as festivals of European films, are organized. The program also includes high quality film content for children and youth. The renovated cinema space of Fontana is the latest display technique, which includes modern digital 3D projectors as well as a classic 35mm film projector.
