Awards

At MARVEL 2018, the following awards and recognitions will be awarded:


AWARDS OF THE MAJOR COMPETITION PROGRAM JUDGES

(International Competition Program):

  •     Award „ MARVEL winner “ for the best film - awarded to the producer 
  •     Special Judges Award 
  •     Award for best direction
  •     Award for the best female role
  •     Award for the best male role
  •     Best Scenario Award

JUDGES AWARDS „ SRPSKI FILM“

( National Competition Program):

  •     Award „ MARVEL winner “ for the best film - awarded to the producer 
  •     Special Judges Award
  •     Award for best direction
  •     Award for the best female role
  •     Award for the best male role
  •     Best Scenario Award

JUDGES AWARDS PROGRAMA "GRANICE"

(subversive,controversial i artistic movies)

  •     Award for the best film - awarded to the producer
  •     Special honor for the best playing film from this program

POLITIKAS AWARD „MILUTIN ČOLIĆ“

Is awarded to the director of the best film in the program „Serbian movie“
JUDGE AWARD FOR FILM CRITICS „NEBOJŠA ĐUKELIĆ“

- Fond „Nebojša Đukelić“ awards the best film of the region
JUDGE AWARD „FEDEORA“

( Federation of film critics from Europe and Mediterranean countries).Two diploms: for the best film in National section and the best Euro-Mediterranean film in main program
MOST WANTED MOVIE AWARD

- Diplom and statue ''Most wanted GORKI LIST movie award'' 
MARVEL AWARDS

-Recognition for outstanding contribution to film art as well as for previous creative work
